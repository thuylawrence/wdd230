Membership Level
Your choice:

NP Membership is for non profit organizations and there is no fee. However, there will are limitations on which activities you can participate in.

Bronze Members will pay a fee of $100 per year. Bronze members will be invited to certain special events and will be able to advertise on the Chamber homepage once a month.

Silver Members will pay a fee of $175 per year. Silver Members will be able to attend certain special events along with training, and will be able to advertise on the Chamber homepage twice a month.

Gold Members will pay a fee of $250. Gold members will be able to attend all events and trainings. The Hanoi Chamber of Commerce homepage will permanent advertising spot for gold members.
